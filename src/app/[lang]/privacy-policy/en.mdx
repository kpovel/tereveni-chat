# Privacy Policy

We, the Tereveni technical team, hereinafter "Tereveni", attach great importance to protecting of privacy and personal data of all users of our web application, hereinafter "Services".

This privacy policy, hereinafter "Privacy Policy", applies to the processing of your personal data as a natural person via our Services and applies to all potential, current and previous users of our Services.

- ### 1. Which personal data is processed, and why?
  
  Only registered users can use our services. By registering for our Services, you confirm that you will respect the privacy of other users.

  During your registration, you have to provide:

   - Login to be used as an identifier within chat groups;

   - Email for login purposes and notifications.

  It is also recommended to add:

   - Photo or avatar;

   - Brief description about yourself.

  All users of Tereveni can only find basic information about other users, which includes  a login, their photo or avatar and a description about the user if such information has  been provided.

- ### 2. What are cookies and how do we use them?
  
  Cookies are text files that are placed on your computer in order to collect standard Internet logging information and behavioural information. This information is used to ensure the correct operation of the web application.

But remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

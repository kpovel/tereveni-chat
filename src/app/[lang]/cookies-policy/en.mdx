# Cookies Policy

We want to be honest and transparent with you about what we do with the data
you give us, so below you can find out:

- What are cookies;
- What cookies do we use;
- When we apply these files;
- For what purpose do we use cookies;
- How you can control their use.

None of our cookies contain information that can be used for your personal
identification. This means that cookies do not store your location, email
address or IP address. They are used exclusively to improve the functionality
of our web application. For more information, visit
[http://aboutcookies.org](http://aboutcookies.org). Our Policy contains
relevant information about the use of cookies, the purpose of their use and
ways to control them.

## 1. What are cookies?

<div className="*:list-none">
- A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit
our chat. This allows the web application to remember your actions and
settings (such as browser language, font size, and other display settings)
over time. This information improves the performance of the web application
and helps us to constantly optimize it.

- Modern web browsers have built-in support for the use of cookies, and most
websites use this tool. Please note that you can always adjust your browser
settings to block specific cookies or receive notifications about their use.
However, be careful when editing cookies settings, as this may lead to
incorrect operation of some elements and functions of the web application.
</div>
